About

Founded in 2017, Novum provides services in Data Analytics & Visualization, and Software Development. For many years, we have been delivering multiple projects, working within local and international markets. Due to increasing demand for process automation and complex data management, we offer customized solutions for each customer to meet their demands, e.g. reporting, agile decision-making, workflow optimization.


Our primary aim is to bring corporate, business and functional level decision-making and reporting to the next level, both visually and process-wise and create data-driven culture.
